### **Collision**
- A situation that occurs when two distinct keys have the same hash value(location)
- Collision Resolution Techniques
- Open Addressing (Closed Hashing)
	- Linear Probing
	- Quadratic Probing
	- Random Probing
	- Double Hashing
- Closed Addressing (Open Hashing)
	- Chaining
